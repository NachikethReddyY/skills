# GLOSSARY.md Format

`GLOSSARY.md` lives at `{topic}/glossary.md`. It is the canonical terminology for that topic. All lessons, records, and references should adhere to its terms. Building it is itself part of learning.

## Structure

```md
# {Topic} Glossary

{One or two sentences describing the topic.}

## Terms

**Term**:
Definition in one or two sentences.
_Avoid_: Wrong or ambiguous alternatives
```

## Rules

- **Add a term only when the user understands it.** The glossary is compressed knowledge, not a dictionary.
- **Be opinionated.** Pick the best term and list aliases to avoid.
- **Keep definitions tight.** Define what the term IS, not what it does.
- **Group under subheadings** when natural clusters emerge.
- **Flag ambiguities explicitly.** "In this workspace, 'set' always means working set."
- **Revise as understanding deepens.** Update in place; don't leave stale entries.
